Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that originated in India. It's a popular choice for special occasions and celebrations. The dish typically consists of marinated chicken, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of spices like cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and saffron.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg boneless, skinless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon saffron strands, soaked in 2 tablespoons warm milk
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or butter
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger, garlic, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt. Set aside for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Wash the basmati rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat ghee or butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add the sliced onion and green chilies. Saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add the marinated chicken to the pot and cook until it is browned on all sides.
  5. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot along with the saffron milk, water, and salt.
  6. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
