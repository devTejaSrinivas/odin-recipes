Vegetable Korma

Description

A rich, creamy, and flavorful dish, Vegetable Korma is a popular Indian curry that features a blend of tender vegetables cooked in a fragrant, cashew-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup cashew nuts, soaked in warm water
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, cauliflower, peas, beans)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté the onion, ginger, and garlic until golden brown.
  2. Add cumin seeds, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and red chili powder. Sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Grind the soaked cashews with yogurt, vegetable broth, and a little water to form a smooth paste.
  4. Pour the cashew-yogurt paste into the pot and bring to a boil.
  5. Add the mixed vegetables and simmer until tender.
  6. Season with salt and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
