Masala Dosa

Description

A crispy, savory crepe filled with a flavorful potato and onion mixture, Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian breakfast dish.

Ingredients

For the Dosa Batter:

  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1 cup idli rice
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed

For the Masala Filling:

  • 2 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

To Make the Dosa Batter:

  1. Wash the urad dal, idli rice, and fenugreek seeds separately.
  2. Soak them in water for 4-5 hours.
  3. Grind the soaked ingredients into a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  4. Ferment the batter for 8-10 hours in a warm place.

To Make the Masala Filling:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add onion, green chili, and ginger. Saut√© until the onion turns golden brown.
  2. Add the mashed potatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Cook for a few more minutes until the masala is well combined.

To Make the Dosa:

  1. Heat a non-stick tawa or griddle.
  2. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the tawa and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin, round crepe.
  3. Cook until the edges turn crisp and golden brown.
  4. Add a spoonful of the masala filling to the center of the dosa.
  5. Fold the edges of the dosa over the filling and serve hot with sambar and coconut chutney.
